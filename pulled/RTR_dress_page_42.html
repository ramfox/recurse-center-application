I ordered this dress in both a 12 and 14. The 12 fit snug, but the 14 was too big. The color is more of a blush pink in person. It was very flattering and people stopped me all night to tell me how lovely my dress was. People of all ages complimented me on it. The reviews on this dress mentioned the seam down the front. Yes it was there but it was not noticeable while being worn at the event. My only real compliant was that the zipper was difficult and would catch. My husband had to help me get in and out after it got pretty stuck when I first tried it my self. I would recommend this dress. The length was great, the fit was great, and the neckline was very modest.
