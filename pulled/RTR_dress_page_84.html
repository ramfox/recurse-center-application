Elegant and comfortable for a formal affair!
This was my first rental from Rent The Runway and I had a great experience! Overall, I loved this Monique Lhuillier dress. It also fell at or just below the knee on me. All in all, I felt sophisticated, comfortable and even a bit regal in this dress. Perfect for a gala or a formal/black tie wedding! Choosing between the 0 and 2: While the 0 was a bit tighter around the ribcage/above the belly button, the 2 was ever so slightly loose beneath the arms but did hang a little nicer around the waist. However, once I sat down, the 2 bunched at the straps/bust a little more. So the 0 it was! It comes down to comfort level and how slim-fitting you prefer to wear your dresses.

This dress was amazing- I didn't want to take it off!
This dress was amazing- I didn't want to take it off! I was taking a risk and trusting the stylist re: the sizing and I'm sure glad I did. I am normally a size 8, but RTR only had a 6 available. The stylist suggested you size down for your back up size and so I took the 6 and hoped for the best (quite the gamble considering I was wearing this to a wedding two days after Thanksgiving!). It fit like a glove! The fabric was heavier than expected, but in a good way (great for a winter wedding). The pockets were clutch- I didn't need a handbag at all! I am pretty short and was worried about the length in the back- was perfectly fine with 3" heels, but when I switched to flats during the reception I had to pick up the back to dance. That was mostly because I didn't want to ruin the pretty dress though, not because I was tripping. I received so many compliments on this dress- everyone loved it and I felt beautiful in it!
