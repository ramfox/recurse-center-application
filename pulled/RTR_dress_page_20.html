Beautiful Dress
After reading the reviews that suggested going up a size, I ordered my usual size 6 and size 8 as a backup. I picked them up from the warehouse (which I will probably never do again) and unpacked them at home. They were packaged nicely, so there were no creases or wrinkles in the fabric. This is a popular dress, so I assume the dress I got had been worn MANY times. The fabric seemed faded, the edging detail was quite worn, to the point that in some areas it was clumping together and the fabric in the back where they eyelet hooks closures are for the neckline was stretched out. There were some holes in the lace that had been repaired with what looked like nylon thread (I only noticed because there was loose thread that I trimmed off four areas). As to the fit of the dress, I wish I had ordered a size down instead of up. If you have narrow shoulders (like me) it will be very loose in that area. I also found it loose in the waist, but fit well through the hip area. I got the regular length since I wore flats, and it was probably an inch or so off the ground. Most people my height (and I imagine anyone wanting to wear 3+ inch heels) would want to go with a long. All in all, it is still a beautiful dress, you can just tell it has been worn a lot. If I use RTR again I think I would stick with a newer dress.
