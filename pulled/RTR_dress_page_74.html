This dress is FANTASTIC. I got so many compliments, and it was SO comfy! The pockets are the best!
I am 5'7", and the length was right at my knees. The sleeves were a little long, but that wasn't a big deal. The sleeves also have small zippers on them that you don't really see in these pictures. The zipper in the back got hung up a little where the fabric changes, but I've seen many dresses do that. Overall, perfect dress for a wedding, and I didn't even have to take a purse because the pockets were so deep!
