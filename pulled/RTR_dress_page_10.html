The red is a beautiful deep berry color. The skirt is full, which was fun for dancing. I definitely felt sexy and stood out from all the little black dresses. The top is a little funny, it puckered out around the arm holes and squished my chest. I didn't wear a bra, but I did feel like I had to keep adjusting the ladies! There is zero give to the waist, which is why I went with the 6 instead of my backup size 4 (which arrived with burn holes it the skirt anyway! Customer service was really helpful in this matter). Overall I would recommend this dress!
