Size 12, 36DD review!
This dress fits true to size and is the same cut and fit as a few other dresses here on RTR by Monigue Lhullier (you can tell them by the high neck and criss cross back). I've rented this one, the navy blue version of it, and the black and white floor length version. They are all beautiful. Out of the three, this one is my least favorite. The lace at the top looks a little bit cheap and although the skirt fabric is heavy enough to hold a nice shape, there is heavier fabric in the hem of the skirt to make it fuller, and the hem was out of shape because of how it was packed and it would have been difficult to press it back into place. The positives: you don't have to wear a bra, it fits really well, the color is beautiful and would be great for a holiday cocktail party.
