Looking for a modern take on a classic lace dress? Look no further! This gorgeous black lace Milly dress is lady-like with a contemporary feel. Simply pair with Lulu Frosts Gold Drift Earrings to complete your look!

This dress runs true to size. We recommend going one size up for back-up (see following fit notes for exceptions). Sized 0-12.
