This has been a second choice dress. I didn’t end up wearing it because my rib cage was too big for the bodice. This dress was absolutely beautiful though and I was dissapointed that I couldn't wear it. Classic and stylish. The high low hem is beautiful along with the full skirt. The material looks like Mikado silk. I really wish I could have worn this dress. I am a 36DD and have a large rib cage but a very small waist. Even though I could get is zipped, I just knew I would be uncomfortable the entire evening if I wore it. I think the 10 would have been too big for the rest of my body or I would attempt to order this again. You won’t be disappointed if you order this gown.
