I wore the 6R (I usually fluctuate between 4-6, but I ordered the 6 and 8 to be safe). I think the 4R would have been fine, though, because the shoulder and waist area was a little loose... but it still fit very nicely (and left room for me to breathe). The sequins are pretty heavy, but the lining was comfortable and it wasn't itchy or anything like that. I would recommend this dress to anyone... I was sad to pack it up to send back!
