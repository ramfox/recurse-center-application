Description

How sweet it is to spend a night twirling across the dance floor in the Black Swan Liana Peach Dress! Soft medium-weight knit starts at a sleeveless, princess-seamed bodice, and continues into a fit-and-flare silhouette. Hidden back zipper.

  • Machine Wash Cold.
