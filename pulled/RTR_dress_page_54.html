I think that this dress was really beautiful.. I got tons of compliments! It is so pretty in person!
This dress was pretty tight. I also ordered the XS just in case, but I couldn't fit it over my butt, lol! I don't have much of a chest, so I wore my sticky bra for some extra padding (had I not, I would not have filled out the cups of the dress). The deep V would have made me self conscious (personally) if my chest was larger, but if you have a larger chest and cleavage is your thing, then this is the dress for you. It's a really beautiful dress in person. I chose to wear it for my husband's police ball, and the theme was 20s and 30s. I received many compliments, and I felt so pretty. (*On a side note, my XS came with pit/deodorant stains/smells, and I was so glad that the Small fit me. This was my first rental, so I didn't know to triple check everything as soon as it arrived. MAKE SURE to open your garment bag and inspect your items thoroughly as soon as you receive them. I didn't do that this time until the night before my event, and had I needed the XS, I would have not been able to get in touch with them in time for a replacement. I did, however, let them know about my issue after the fact, and RTR took care of it with excellent customer service!)
